Why Healthcare Security Matters

Healthcare systems are a prime target for cybercriminals. Globally, the healthcare industry lags behind other sectors in cybersecurity measures, making it particularly vulnerable to attacks. In Nigeria specifically, digital health adoption has been slow: many facilities still rely on paper-based records, and those that implement digital records lack comprehensive IT protection.

The Risk

Healthcare is now the #1 target for cybercriminals With the rise of digital health records, remote consultations, and interconnected medical devices, the healthcare industry has become a goldmine for cyber attackers.

The Impact

Attacks cause system downtime and risk patient safety.  Cyberattacks in healthcare aren’t just about stolen data — they can interrupt life-saving care. Ransomware can shut down entire hospital systems, delay treatments, and make patient records inaccessible in emergencies

The Solution

Smart cybersecurity is no longer a luxury — it’s essential.  To stay ahead of today’s threats, healthcare providers need modern, adaptive, and AI-driven security systems. From real-time monitoring to zero-trust architecture, effective cybersecurity must be proactive and scalable..
